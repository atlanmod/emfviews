Build your own model views.

Some users only need to see some parts of a model, others have to get the full model extended with data from another model, and others simply access to a combination of information coming from different models.

Think of database views.

Based on the unquestionable success/usefulness of database views to solve similar problems in databases, EMF Views aims to bring the same concept to the modeling world.
